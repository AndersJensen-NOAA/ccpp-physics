\section des_GFDL_cloud Description
Unique attributes of GFDL microphysics:
1. \b Fast \b physics (phase changes only, for now) between "Lagrangian-to-Eulerian" remapping in FV3
2. \b Time-split between warm-rain (faster) and ice-phase (slower) processes
3. \b Time \b implicit monotonic scheme for terminal fall of condensates
4. \b Thermodynamic \b consistency between the dynamics and cloud microphysics:
  - exact local moist energy conservation between phase changes
  - condensates carry heat & 3D momentum
5. \b Scale-awareness achieved by an assumed horizontal subgrid variability and a 2nd
order FV-type vertical reconstruction (\cite lin_et_al_1994) for autoconversions (ice->snow)
